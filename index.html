Created by four men eager to protect the enviornment, NSS Zoo was made with the sole importance of conservation in mind. All our facilities are state of the art, carbon nuetral enviornments with animal care at the fore front. Not only does NSS Zoo care for its own animals, but with climate change on the rise, NSS has taken a position at the front defense by contributing a large portion of our proceeds directly to global conservation efforts. We hope you join us one day at the Zoo to see our amazing and unique animals!

Fun Facts About our Zoo!

  • Brand new to Nashville, NSS Zoo was created by our founders Tom Clouse, Devin Garcia, Noah Husain, & Jonathan Watson in April of 2021.
  • Committed to the increasing needs of suitable housing for exotic animals, NSS Zoo has start of the art technology to ensure that our animals are kept comfortable and safe during their time with us.
  • During the initial planning of our company, our four founders shattered the industry standard of using sketchbook to form the design of our site and instead drew all our plans on a microsoft paint file. This file can be found in our main hall.
  • Quickly becoming of the hottest locations in Nashville, NSS Zoo has seen some of the largest growth nationwide when compared to other zoo's.
  • Charitable donations are nothing new to NSS Zoo as 20% of our proceeds go directly to wildlife conservation efforts around the globe ranging from the plains of Europe to the sahara's of Africa.
